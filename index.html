Transaction Type Guide

Intra-state

Buyer and seller are in the same state. GST is split equally: CGST (Central) + SGST (State).

₹10,000 @ 18% → CGST ₹900 + SGST ₹900 = ₹1,800
Inter-state

Buyer and seller are in different states, or transaction involves import/export. Only IGST applies — CGST & SGST are ₹0.

₹10,000 @ 18% → IGST ₹1,800 (CGST = SGST = ₹0)
Union Territory (no legislature)

Applies in Ladakh, Andaman & Nicobar, Chandigarh, Dadra & NH, Daman & Diu, Lakshadweep. CGST + UTGST (instead of SGST).

₹10,000 @ 18% → CGST ₹900 + UTGST ₹900 = ₹1,800

Delhi, J&K, and Puducherry have their own legislatures, so SGST (not UTGST) applies there.
